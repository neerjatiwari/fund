Welcome to my Triple Crown for Heart fundraising page!

On July 15th I will tackle all three North Shore Mountains (Seymour, Grouse and Cypress) by bike in the 11th annual Triple Crown for Heart. The ride benefits the BC Children's Hospital Heart Unit and Children's Heart Network to send kids to Camp Zajac. I would be grateful for your sponsorship and pledge of faith that I can do this!

Donations of $20 or more will receive a charitable donation tax receipt at the end of the year. Thanks for your consideration of support for this most worthy cause!
